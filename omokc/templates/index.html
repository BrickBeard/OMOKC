What is Orofacial Myology?

The study and treatment of oral and facial muscles as they relate to speech, dentition, chewing/bolus collection, swallowing and overall mental and physical health.

- Sandra Holtzman

What We Do:

OMOKC uses exercises to change muscle patterns and rest postures of the tongue and lips. Therapy usually takes 3-5 minutes per day for about 8-10 weeks.
